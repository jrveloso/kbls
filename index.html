About Us

KBLS Corp. is a small veteran owned company established in April 2022. This small company, based out of Chicago Illinois, provides an array of services for the logistics community. Our team of professionals are there to service your every logistical need and support even the toughest of requirements. We are the company to call when other companies fail to show up and cover your requirements.

Some of our specialties and expertise are expedited/last minute freight, super cargo, and deployment/re-deployment of large volume requirements. We operate in multiple facets of logistics with outstanding experience in open-deck freight. We will service any requirement that you have and guarantee that we exceed your expectations. We run this company with honor and integrity and will not let your mission fail. We will get it done.
